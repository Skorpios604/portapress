The Punch Tools Corner Notcher is the ultimate tool for notching corners in sheet metal with one of 4 outside radii angles (1/2", 3/4", 1", and 1.5" angles).

Quick and easy to use, the Punch Tools Corner Notcher is not only versitile, but dependable for large-volume work with a single operator.
